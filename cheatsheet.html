First Time Hacker Cheat Sheet


TL;DR: Bring a friend and plan to stay the night



Hack? Is this legal?
  • "Hacking" has a bad rep in the media, but here we're talking about making something cool in a short amount of time with limited resources (think "life hack")

Gear:
  • a friend (preferably at a similar skill level)
  • toothbrush, computer, charger, comfortable clothes, your brain
  • optional: extension cord, dry erase marker, sticky notes, pen + paper (we will have some at the event, but may not be able to give to everyone), change of clothes

Designer vs Hacker?
  • usually takes longer to build the tech than to design, so we will also have mini design competitions at night to keep designers on their toes
  • Facebook portfolio review
  • you can be both and still submit to the design contests

Schedule:
    Coming soon!

What kind of projects can I make?
  • anything! everything from a website that tells your grandma to get off the computer to your own programming language
  • a lot of the best projects are either really serious and impressive, or really creative, or really funny jokes

How do prizes work?
  • First place / runner up: decided by the organizers + sponsors
  • sponsor specific: best use case of their API
  • people's choice: we will have a voting system after the event for people to choose their favorite that they think didn't get an award
  • Accolades: the organizers have picked a handful of badges

  • they are strictly for the sponsors, who want to give you jobs
